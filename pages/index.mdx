# Anuar Ustayev

Hey, I'm a Product Lead and Senior Software Engineer at [Datopian](https://www.datopian.com/).

For the last few years I've been helping governments, NGOs and enterprises to build data catalogues, internal data management systems and open data portals.

Engineered, consulted and/or lead projects:

- US General Services Administration: [Data.Gov](https://www.data.gov/)
- UK National Electricity System Operator: [data.nationalgrideso.com](https://data.nationalgrideso.com/)
- UK National Health Service: [opendata.nhsbsa.net](https://opendata.nhsbsa.net/)
- Denmark Electricity and Natural Gas Operator: [energidataservice.dk](https://www.energidataservice.dk/) and [energidataportal.dk](https://www.energidataportal.dk/)
- Denmark Open Data Portal: [opendata.dk](https://www.opendata.dk/)
- Also involved in other national level projects in US, Canada, Singapore, UAE, Switzerland, Germany and many more.

To see my latest open-source contributions have a look at [**my github profile**](https://github.com/anuveyatsu).
